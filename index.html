Who I Am

I was born and raised in Fargo, North Dakota, and from an early age, I was fascinated by the unknown. Whether I was searching for strange bugs in my backyard or tinkering with my mom’s computer and phone, I always wanted to understand how things worked. That same curiosity led me to coding. I started by teaching myself HTML and CSS, and later, I joined Emerging Digital Academy’s bootcamp to expand my skills. There, I discovered my passion for building web applications—turning ideas into reality through code.
